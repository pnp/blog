## Happy holidays and successful year 2026!

On behalf of our [virtual community team](https://aka.ms/community/team), I want to thank everyone for an awesome 2025 and hopefully you all will have an opportunity to charge batteries during the holiday season.

On the community side, 2025 was a year of growth in many areas. Our community engagement model is based on helping each and every community member to achieve more within their careers and daily work. 

**Huge thanks for everyone** who have been involved on the different community areas organized by our team or any other community organization. Key is to **get involved** and help others - as sharing is caring!

Few key metrics from 2025 on our community work:

* \>29,000 live [community call](https://aka.ms/community/calls) attendees
* 111 live [community calls](https://aka.ms/community/calls) focused on Microsoft 365 & Power Platform
* \>70,000 subscribers in the [Microsoft Community Learning](https://aka.ms/community/youtube) YouTube channel
* \>2M views in the in the [Microsoft Community Learning](https://aka.ms/community/youtube) YouTube channel during 2025
* \>2,600 samples in the [sample gallery](https://aka.ms/community/samples) from Microsoft and community members
