We are excited to announce that we are growing our virtual [Microsoft 365 & Power Platform Community coordination team](https://aka.ms/community/team) with additional community members. This virtual community team is responsible of the different [Microsoft 365 & Power Platform Community](https://aka.ms/community/home) activities in different community channels, including our open-source work in the GitHub. 

This team consists of Microsoft employees and community members (MVPs) **focused on helping the community** with the best use of Microsoft products, like Microsoft Copilot, Microsoft Teams, Microsoft Viva, Power Platform, OneDrive, SharePoint, or API layer like Microsoft Graph.

We believe that by **working together as one unified team across the organization barriers**, we can make even larger worldwide impact and help other community members to succeed in adopting different practices within Microsoft 365 & Power Platform areas. **Better together with shared objectives**, not in isolation.

We are **always looking for active contributors and coordinators on this team**. If you are interested in getting involved, reach out to team members and we will find you a suitable path to support your journey.

*“We are committed on building the best tech community in the world where everyone feels welcome, and we help to empower each and every community member to achieve more.” 🧡*

Thank you for being part of this incredible community!

## Ejaz Hussain - Technical Architect | Microsoft MVP

![Ejaz Hussain](images/ejaz.png)

Ejaz Hussain is a Technical Architect at [Advania UK](https://www.advania.co.uk/), based in London, with over **15 years of experience** designing and delivering enterprise solutions across **Microsoft 365, Azure, and the Power Platform**. He began his career as a SharePoint developer and has evolved into a solution architect specializing in **Copilot extensibility, intelligent agents, and enterprise-scale cloud architecture**.
 
With a strong focus on **Microsoft Copilot, AI agents, and Microsoft Graph**, Ejaz works at the intersection of Generative AI and real‑world business solutions—building secure, scalable architectures that integrate enterprise knowledge, automation, and collaboration. His work spans **SPFx, Azure AI Foundry, Azure OpenAI, custom Copilot skills, Graph connectors, and Power Platform integrations**, with a strong emphasis on responsible AI and governance.
 
Ejaz is an active community contributor and regular speaker on **Microsoft 365 & Power Platform Community calls**, as well as a contributor of samples and guidance to **PnP repositories**. He frequently speaks at global conferences, including the **European Power Platform Conference**, sharing practical insights on Copilot APIs, agentic architectures, and production‑ready extensibility patterns.

As a **Microsoft MVP**, Ejaz is passionate about giving back to the community. 
 
Joining the **PnP Core Team** allows him to continue supporting developers and architects worldwide through hands‑on guidance, real‑world samples, and architectural best practices, helping organizations adopt Microsoft 365, Copilot, and AI with confidence.

## Marcin Wojciechowski - Head of Competency | Microsoft MVP

![Marcin Wojciechowski](images/marcin.png)

Marcin Wojciechowski is a Microsoft MVP in M365 Development and Head of Competency at [Avenga](https://www.avenga.com/), where he leads **Microsoft 365 and Dynamics 365 expertise** within the Global Expertise Center. With more than a decade of experience working with **SharePoint and Microsoft 365 technologies**, he focuses on delivering scalable, enterprise-grade solutions and driving technical excellence across teams.

Marcin is a passionate advocate for **high-quality software engineering practices**, with a strong emphasis on clean architecture and test-driven development. Beyond his professional role, he is an active contributor to the Microsoft community—sharing insights through his [blog](https://mgwdevcom.wordpress.com/) and maintaining the open-source library [mgwdev-m365-helpers](https://github.com/mgwojciech/mgwdev-m365-helpers). He regularly speaks at industry events, supporting knowledge sharing and innovation across the ecosystem.
